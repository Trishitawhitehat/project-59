My Favourite Hobby

Painting

My Hobby is painting. I am 10 years old and
read in class 4th standard. Whenever Sunday comes I like to
draw some scenery and many other things. I love painting because Painting
is a form of expression, if done right, it can show plethora of emotions. Each color has a
significance and sometimes, people want to express through paintings, rather than words. De-stressing
mechanism: Science says that painting can help us reduce stress and may develop happy hormones in the body.
